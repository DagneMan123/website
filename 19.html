Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental services

What do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need a valid driver's license, a major credit card in your name, and proof of insurance if you're declining our coverage. Renters must be at least 21 years old (25 for certain vehicle classes).

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time. For last-minute changes, please contact our customer service team.

What's included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include unlimited mileage, basic liability coverage, and 24/7 roadside assistance. Optional extras like additional insurance, GPS, and child seats are available for an additional fee.

Can I rent a car one-way?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between most of our locations. Additional fees may apply depending on the drop-off location. Please check availability when making your reservation.

What is your fuel policy?

Our standard policy is "full-to-full" where you receive the car with a full tank and return it full. We also offer a prepaid fuel option where you pay for a full tank upfront and can return the car at any fuel level.
